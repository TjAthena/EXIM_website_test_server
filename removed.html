JAVA Peanuts

Java peanuts are smaller, uniquely pale-skinned peanuts with a mild flavor and dense texture, ideal for confectionery and baking uses.

TJ Peanuts

TJ peanuts are known for their high oil content, making them perfect for oil extraction, roasting, and snack production industries.

Bold Peanuts

Bold peanuts are larger, reddish-skinned peanuts with a strong flavor and crunchy texture, widely used in roasted snacks and peanut butter.

Blanched

Blanched peanuts are peanuts with the skins removed, often used in cooking, baking, and as a base for peanut butter and confectionery.
