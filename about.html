Welcome To SHATAPATHRAM EXIM OPC PVT LTD

We’ve been in the global peanut business for over 4.5 years. And we handle more than 150 tons of peanuts a year. So from farming to multi-location processing and value-added products, we know the market.

As peanut visionaries, we’re determined to build a better industry for farmers, buyers, distributors, value-adders, manufacturers, and consumers. That’s why we rigorously pursue excellence in everything we do, working to transform the peanut industry for the better.
